Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FT Timer handles user data.

1. Data Collection

FT Timer does not collect, store, or process any personal data.
The app does not require user accounts, logins, or profile creation.

2. Usage Data

FT Timer does not track user activity, analytics, or behavioral data.

3. Purchases

All in-app purchases and subscription payments are handled securely by Apple’s App Store using Apple’s In-App Purchase system.
FT Timer does not have access to payment information, billing details, or transaction data.

4. Third-Party Services

FT Timer does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on the user’s device and are never transmitted to external servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

FT Timer does not knowingly collect data from children and is suitable for general audiences.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
